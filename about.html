About Salon K7

Welcome to Salon K7, where beauty meets expertise! Located in the heart of Kalugamuwa, Kiralabokka, Kurunegala, our salon is dedicated to providing exceptional beauty services that enhance your natural beauty and boost your confidence.

Under the skilled hands of Krishan Ranathunga, we offer a complete range of professional salon services in a warm, welcoming environment. Our commitment to excellence and attention to detail ensures that every client leaves feeling refreshed, styled, and beautiful.

At Salon K7, we believe that everyone deserves to look and feel their best. Whether you're preparing for a special occasion or simply treating yourself to some well-deserved pampering, we're here to make your beauty dreams come true.

Visit us today and experience the difference that professional care and genuine passion for beauty can make!
